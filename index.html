What is a problem you see?

Collaboration

Syner connects people together through the common problems that they face. Using shared editing features we allows groups to cumulatively define and build suggested solutions to problems that we witness.

Anonymous Reporting

Report problems that you see in the world without tying them to your social identity. If you choose to be notified when content you submitted has been modified, we'll send you an email without giving out your personal information to anyone.

Fast & Simple

We believe simplicity and ease of use are values to uphold over total feature capability. We want to make sure our software is reliable and serves a core purpose before accepting a multitude of feature implementations.

Transparency

We believe trust is the most pivotal value to uphold for anyone that users Syner. We share our business practices and spending habits in praise of transparency so that the community understands our status and can offer incite into improving our own functionality.

Reputation & Rating

While anonymous content submission is great for getting ideas and reported problems on the table. We still need community leaders to curate content and they should be recognized for the work that they do. We employ game mechanics that allows users to unlock functionality in the system to prevent new users from ruining quality/establish content.
