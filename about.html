Core Concepts

  • Speed & Efficiency - They should be able to submit their feedback from one page, load it from one URL, and be done. Report the problem and get back to life.. if they choose to.
  • Minimalism + Ease Of Use - Don't ask for a survey, give them one text field. Don't ask for personal information, we value their message more than the exchange.
  • Collaborative Curation - Revision history is important, but we need markup that is easier than wiki style; a la Medium. Allow users to add/reject each-others tag suggestions or URL attachments.
  • Transparency - Share business practices, logged data (that doesn't share personal information), expenses and revenue streams. Show the true status of the project. Why hide it?
  • Open Source - You can take the source code and run your own Syner site for your organization. Let them make it extensive, or customize it to their industrial needs. We want to be the backbone of development but can still prosper from their innovations. Our specific value proposition is easy to setup and manage hosted solutions, not controlling the source.
  • Open Feedback
  • Small Teams + Few People Produce High Quality Work
  • Through total Quantity we also find quality
  • Embrace the 80/20 rule of thumb.
